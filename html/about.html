Missouri (1977-1986)

I was born in St. Charles in 09/1977. Eventually my famliy moved to St. Peters in 1980 and stayed there until 1986.

Illinois (1986-1991)

In 1986 we move to Carterville with a population of around 5000 people in the town. Carterville is about halfway between Marion, off I-57, and Carbondale. Carbondale had and still has Southern Ilinois University, SIU. During my time in Carterville my dad and I went to alot of SIU football and basketball games. In 1991 we moved to Kentucky.

Kentucky (1991-Now)

In 1991 we moved to Louisville where we remain today, sort of. Like everyone else, we are slowly moving farther away from each other. My older sister and her husband live in North Carolina. I started high school and went to college here in Louisville. While living here I have gone to multiple University of Louisville football and basketball games as well as nemerous minor league baseball and hockey games. as well as traveling to Cincinatti, Indianapolis, St. Louis, and many other cities and towns for MLB, NFL, Milb, and independent baseball games. As well as a few NFL games and a few Indy 500 races. Living here I have also been to Chirchill Downs for a few horse races as well as one Kentucky Derby.
